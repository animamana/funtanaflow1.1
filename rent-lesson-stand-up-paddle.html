Stand Up Paddle

offerta fino al 30/06

The Stand Up Paddle is the perfect way to discover the coast of Sardinia from a different perspective, immersed in nature and away from mass tourism. In the Sinis Peninsula, between Funtana Meiga, Cabras, Is Arutas and the coast of Oristano, SUP becomes a unique experience, accessible to all and unforgettable. Paddling standing on a board allows you to explore hidden coves, white quartz beaches and stretches of clear sea where the water blends with the sky. It is an easy sport to learn, suitable for all ages, and perfect for those seeking movement, tranquility, and authentic contact with the landscape. With Funtana Flow you can choose to experience it on your own or rely on an experienced guide.

We offer:

High quality individual or group SUP board rentals

Individual or group lessons with certified instructors

Advice and assistance on the best routes based on wind and sea conditions

Guided experiences among beaches, ponds and cliffs of Sinis

Every SUP outing in Sinis is different: you can start from Funtana Meiga and paddle to Capo San Marco, cross pristine areas and spot flamingos near the ponds of Mistras. Far from the crowds, close to the essence of Sardinia.

Book your Stand Up Paddle experience with Funtana Flow now. Whether you are a beginner or experienced practitioner, you will find a welcoming environment, professional equipment, and the perfect opportunity to experience the sea in freedom.
