Kayak

offerta fino al 30/06

Discover the Sinis Peninsula from a privileged vantage point: the sea. With a kayak excursion between Funtana Meiga, Tharros, Capo San Marco and the lagoons of Cabras, you can experience the coast of western Sardinia in an active, sustainable and fully immersive way. Paddling along clear and calm waters, hidden coves, breathtaking views and archaeological remains overlooking the sea await you. Between sea and ponds, nature and history come together in a landscape that surprises with every stroke of the paddle. Kayaking is a sport accessible to everyone: adults, kids, families, beginners or experienced sportsmen. It is the perfect choice for those seeking movement, adventure and direct contact with nature.

With Funtana Flow you can:

Rent single or double kayaks and set off independently, with briefing and map

Participate in guided excursions along routes between open sea, reefs and lagoons

Receive support, assistance and advice for paddling safely and freely

All equipment is of high quality, designed to provide you with comfort and safety. Our team will guide you in choosing the best route based on wind and sea conditions, so you can have an authentic and tailored experience.

Whether you want to paddle at sunset in front of the ruins of Tharros or explore the wild coastline to Is Arutas, kayaking is the ideal way to discover the hidden magic of Sinis.

Book your kayaking experience now with Funtana Flow and be surprised by the truest Sardinia.
